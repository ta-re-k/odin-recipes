Risotto

Description :

Risottois a creamy Italian dish made with short-grain rice cooked in broth until perfectly tender. It's versatile, with variations featuring mushrooms, seafood, or even saffron for a luxurious touch. Risotto requires constant stirring to release the rice's starch, creating its signature velvety texture.

The process starts by sautéing onions in butter or olive oil, adding rice, and lightly toasting it. Gradually, warm broth is added while stirring, allowing the rice to absorb the liquid. Once cooked, it's finished with parmesan cheese and a touch of butter for extra creaminess. Risotto is a modern comfort food that's rich, satisfying, and endlessly customizable.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth (kept warm)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: mushrooms, saffron, shrimp, or asparagus

Steps :

  1. Heat oil/butter and sauté onions until soft. Add rice and stir until lightly toasted.
  2. Add the rice and stir until lightly toasted
  3. Gradually add warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until the liquid is absorbed. Repeat until the rice is tender and creamy (about 20 minutes).
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese, season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
